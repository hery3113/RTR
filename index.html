How To Get TRX?
You can easily acquire TRX from most popular cryptocurrency exchange platforms, like Binance, Indodax, Bitfinex, OKEx, etc. After purchasing TRX, you can transfer it to your personal wallet, and then transfer it to our smart-contract address.
Binance Indodax
IMPORTANT: We are only working with personal wallets. Please don’t make deposits directly fromthe exchange platforms. If you do so, the payouts will be made to the exchange platform and not to your account - you will lose funds. As such, whenever you want to send TRX payments to our smart-contract address, please route it through your personal wallet.
